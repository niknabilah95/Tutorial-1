UNIVERSITI MALAYSIA PERLIS

Dean' Message Universiti Malaysia Perlis (UniMAP) was established as a Public Institution of Higher Education (IPTA) on 25 July 2001. The establishment of UniMAP aims to produce highly skilled and competent engineers to fulfill and support the National Industrialization Plan. Curriculum development activities, physical development and staff recruitment began after the appointment of the Vice-Chancellor and Deputy Vice-Chancellor on 16 February 2002. UniMAP began operating at a temporary campus in Kubang Gajah, Arau, Perlis on 1 February 2007. While the first intake of 116 students for the 2002/2003 academic session was made on 20 June 2002.UniMAP implements teaching and learning in accordance with the EAC-BEM accreditation requirements toproduce engineers who are capable and able to apply any theoretical concept. UniMAP's academic program is based on approximately 80% teaching in the form of theory and 20% practical.The curriculum provided has been designed to be equivalent to any engineering program recognized locally and abroad.UniMAP's permanent campus is in Pauh,covering an area of 1050 acres,and in Sungai Chuchuh, covering an area of 300 acres, and is still in the development stage. However, there are several Departments and Schools that are fully operational at the Pauh Putra Campus, including the Library Department,School of Mechatronics Engineering, School of Microelectronics Engineering, School of Manufacturing Engineering, School of Computer & Communication Engineering, School of Electrical Systems Engineering and Institute of Engineering Mathematics.
